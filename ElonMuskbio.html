Life of Elon Musk

Early Life

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa. He spent his early childhood with his brother Kimbal and sister Tosca in South Africa. When he was 10 years old his parents divorced. Since then he had an interest in computers and computer programming. At the age of 17, in 1989, he moved to Canada to attend Queen’s University. Later, in 1992, he left to study business and physics at the University of Pennsylvania. He graduated with an undergraduate degree in economics and stayed for a second bachelor’s degree in physics. He then went to Stanford University in California to get a PhD in energy physics, however he dropped out of Stanford in just two days to launch his first company, Zip2 Corporation. This was an online city guide that provided content for the new websites of the New York Times and the Chicago Tribune. In 1999, a division of Compaq Computer Corporation bought Zip2 for $307 million in cash and $34 million in stock options. This was the start of Elon Musk and his journey consisting of being a part of many major companies, as we know today, including:

  • PayPal
  • X.com
  • SpaceX
  • Tesla Motors
  • SolarCity

Quick facts
